# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect all participants in this organization to communicate with respect, focus on facts, and collaborate in good faith.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive in reviews and discussions.
- Challenge ideas, not people.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, or hostile language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after feedback.

## Scope

This applies in repositories, pull requests, issues, discussions, and other project communication channels.

## Reporting

Report concerns to: `security@alpininsight.com`.

Include links, timestamps, and relevant context so maintainers can investigate quickly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict permissions, or remove access based on severity and repeated violations.
